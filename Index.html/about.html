About Us

Our Mission

We are committed to making home design accessible, inclusive, and eco-friendly. Our platform offers AI-powered design recommendations that cater to different user needs, whether you're a bachelor, family, specially-abled, or someone looking for a universal design. We aim to revolutionize the way people think about home design while considering various factors like budget, style, accessibility, and sustainability.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower individuals to create beautiful, functional, and inclusive spaces with the help of advanced technologies. We strive to bring people together through design, ensuring everyone has the ability to personalize their living spaces regardless of their abilities or limitations.

Features

  • AI-based Recommendations: Get personalized home design suggestions based on your preferences and needs.
  • 3D Model Visualization: Preview your designs in real-time using AR for a realistic experience.
  • Accessibility Focus: Inclusive design options for specially-abled people, ensuring everyone feels at home.
  • Sustainable Materials: Eco-friendly material recommendations to reduce environmental impact.
